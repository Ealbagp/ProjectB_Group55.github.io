Did You Know?

How does PM2.5 affect children?

Fine particulate matter can penetrate deep into children's lungs, impairing development and triggering inflammation—especially among those with pre-existing asthma.

What neighborhoods are most at risk?

Historically redlined and industrial-adjacent areas like the South Bronx and parts of Northern Manhattan experience higher pollution and asthma burdens.

What is the Clean Heat Program?

NYC’s Clean Heat Program phases out the use of heavy residual oil in buildings, reducing emissions of soot and sulfur dioxide. Learn more.

What Is Asthma?

Asthma is a chronic respiratory disease that causes inflammation and narrowing of the airways. Common symptoms include wheezing, shortness of breath, chest tightness, and coughing.

Triggers include air pollutants, allergens, cold air, stress, and respiratory infections.
